# General Information

We collect limited personally identifiable information (“data”) from Github, and aggregate information on what pages users visit. This information is solely used to provide our services and improve their quality. No data is shared with other organizations for commercial purposes; it is only used to provide services and improve quality. We only share data when you provide us with permission to do so, except in cases where data is necessary to take legal action, or in the case of GDCS being transferred to another business.

# Data Collection and Usage

We collect the following types of information:
- Users who directly edit website pages using our content management system (CMS), available through any “edit this page” links, must authenticate using GitHub. This information is stored locally and does not leave your device. You can remove it by signing out of the CMS, or by clearing your cookies.
- Aggregate user information (device type, country of origin, and usage statistics) are collected for the purpose of analytics. This information is shared with Google Analytics to monitor website use and make improvements to the site. You consent to this information being collected when you enable analytics cookies in <a href="#" data-cc="show-preferencesModal">cookie settings</a>. You may also withdraw your consent at any time, and we will promptly stop collecting analytics data about your device.
- User search information (such as search terms, results, and any website interactions as a result of searches) are also collected for analytics purposes. This information is shared with Algolia Docsearch to monitor website use and make improvements to the site. This information is also used to retain your search history for your own personal use. This is necessary to provide the website’s search feature. You may remove this information by clearing your cookies or avoiding the search feature altogether.
- Cookies are used to provide additional website functionality. These may include aesthetic cookies, such as remembering your choice of light or dark mode. Some of the data described above also uses cookies. You can control how the site uses cookies by configuring your browser settings. Be aware that disabling cookies entirely may limit certain website functionality.

We do not collect any information that is not described above.

## Data Retention and Security

We keep your data for the time necessary to provide our services. Some information, such as aggregate information, is stored with our third-party vendors for up to 14 months.

We take reasonable actions to ensure your data is secure and prevent it from unauthorized access, sharing, or modification. Be aware that no security actions are 100% foolproof, and we cannot ensure complete security for your data.

## Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to request a copy of your data, or that it be updated, deleted, or restricted. We will comply with all reasonable data requests unless any applicable laws restrict us from doing so, or identifying, updating, or removing your data poses a significant burden.

In accordance with the European Union’s GDPR law, we let you control if any analytics data is collected by us. Any other data we collect is necessary for site functionality. If you do not want any data or cookies to be created, you should stop using this site entirely.

You may control collection of analytics data by using our <a href="#" data-cc="show-preferencesModal">Cookie Settings</a>.

# Links

We may have content which links to third-party sites. These sites are subject to their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for any content or data collection they use. Please understand that clicking on these links will take you away from the GDCS website, and any personal data you provide to third-party sites is not covered by or subject to this privacy policy.

# Changes

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Please review the policy periodically for the latest information as necessary.
